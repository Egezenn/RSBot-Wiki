# Inventory tab

Lets you see what you have in inventory, pets, storages and use the items in your inventory.

> DEPRECATED IMAGE
>
> Sorting options are added and the available space in your inventory can be seen at the bottom

> You can right-click an item to use & drop / move to pickpet / set conditions for usage.
